LOCATION CANNOT BE IGNORED

The neighborhood is full of rows of customers to give customers a great experience, enjoy delicious, cheap street food and ensure safety and hygiene. Check in with your friends for a great neighborhood.

FAIR NIGHT HELIO

THE FOOD STREAM ABOUT DA NANG'S LARGEST NIGHT

As a famous night market located on the central road of September 2 in Da Nang, not only locals and tourists alike, Helio night market is the place you need; a place with hundreds of delicious dishes from snacks, satiety, seafood buffet, beer, barbeque, milk tea, juice .... Make sure you want to eat anything, Helio night market has it all! Operating every day from 17h30 to 22h30, with more than 150 food stalls, divided into 3 main sections, the cuisine at Helio night market is the attraction that makes Helio night market more prominent than other places.

CHỢ CỒN

PLACE OF DISABILIZATION OF DISABILITY OF NANG DA NANG

Going to Da Nang and ignoring Con market is indeed a waste of money. Con Market is located at the corner of Hung Vuong and Ong Ich Khiem streets, right in the heart of the city, so it's easy for tourists to find, this is also a destination with countless delicious dishes to try..

CHỢ BẮC MỸ

Places to eat and drink cheap

When it comes to the heaven of delicacies that are surprisingly cheap, we can just name Bac My An Market right away. The all-day meeting market located in Ngu Hanh Son district is considered by the Da Nang "colonies" as a dining paradise with a lot of delicious and strange dishes, but the price is too cheap.
